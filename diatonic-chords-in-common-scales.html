Chapter 2 Diatonic Chords in Common Scales

This section lists triads and chords in the Major, and Minor scales. Chords in these scales are constructed using intervals of 3rds. E.g., in the C major scale (C, D, E, F, G, A, B), the C major 7 chord is constructed using C, E, G, B.
