1 Diatonic Chords in Major and Minor Scales

This chapter contains information about chords (built in 3rds) in following scales: Major, Natural Minor, Harmonic Minor, and Melodic Minor.

Scale Formula Nums 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
Chord Roman Nums I ii iii IV V vi vii°
Functional Names Tonic Supertonic Mediant Subdominant Dominant Submediant Leading Tone
Triads maj min min maj maj min dim
7th Chords maj7 m7 m7 maj7 dom7 min7 m7b5
Example in C Cmaj7 Dm7 Em7 Fmaj7 G7 Am7 Bm7b5
